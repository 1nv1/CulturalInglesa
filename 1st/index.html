Sentenes: Examples

  1. He met a stranger.
  2. Did Sheila hear me on the phone? Yes, she did.
  3. Tom and Sally watched TV all afternoon last Saturday.
  4. You didn't pay the waiter last night. I paid (pay) it!
  5. The boys didn't play play basketball at school a year ago.
  6. My daughter liked the book. It was a great present for her.
  7. Alice wasn't married when you met her for the first time.
  8. Did you saw Eirc last class? No, I didn't.
  9. Was Harry at home yesterday? No, he wasn't.
  10. We were very happy to see you last year.
  11. Lunch was great yesterday, but dinner wasn't. Just a sandwich!
  12. I can't find my keys. The were here this morning.
  13. Yesterday Mr. Brown went fishing at the lake.
  14. They had lunch together at a restaurant.
  15. Tom and Sally played together all weekend.
  16. On Sunday Mark read a book about dinosaurs.
  17. I didn't start work at 9a.m. yesterday.
  18. Did you ask your teacher about the exam? asked -> ast
  19. I didn't see the film you recommend. I don't have time.
  20. Did you buy the oranges for the fruit salad?
